Hi! I'm a recent Ph.D. graduate of Liverpool John Moores University's Astrophysics Research Institute. I'm currently taking some time away from the field to explore data-driven opportunities in industry, but have plans to return soon.

My research interests include: disentangling the quenching and the morphological transformation of galaxies, the role of environment and mergers in galaxy evolution, decomposing the morphologies and the spectral energy distributions of galaxies, and applications of machine learning techniques.
