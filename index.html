Hi. I'm a Senior Analyst at Research England, UKRI. I help to deliver the annual funding round and deliver other ad-hoc analytical work as well.

I trained as an astrophysicist, completing my Ph.D. at Liverpool John Moores University's Astrophysics Research Institute in 2020. I also had a brief post-doctoral stint at Tartu Observatory in Estonia in 2022. My main research interests were galaxies, their evolution, their environments, and applications of unsupervised machine learning techniques.
